## What gets featured

We pick contributions that:

- **Unlock something** — open a workflow that wasn't easy before
- **Are well-documented** — anyone can read the file and understand
- **Show range** — different from what we've featured before
- **Have run for real** — the author has used them on actual work

## How to nominate

You can nominate your own work or someone else's:

1. **Self-nomination** — mention "featured-candidate" in your PR description when you submit.
2. **Nominating another contributor** — open a Discussion titled `Showcase nomination: <slug>` with a link to their PR and one paragraph on why.

We're transparent about selection — if we don't pick yours, we'll tell you what we'd want to see refined.

## Beyond the showcase: the hall

When the community library passes 100 contributions, we'll start a "Hall" — a permanent list of every contributor + their work — with a short bio if they want one.

Until then: each release notes the contributors who shipped that release. Look there for the running list.

## Contributor benefits

- Featured contributors get a **complimentary Pro license** for 12 months (one per cycle).
- After 3 featured contributions, a complimentary **Teams license** (5 seats) for your team or open-source project.
- Annual community thank-you post on `azmx.ai/blog`.

(These are gestures, not bounties — we'd rather you build what excites you than chase a feature slot.)

## Anti-fraud

We don't accept "bulk submissions" gaming the showcase — one polished contribution beats five rushed ones every time. We reserve the right to skip a contribution if the work doesn't match the standard, regardless of submission timing.
