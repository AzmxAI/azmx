# Rubber duck

The classic — explain a stuck problem to inanimate-object-but-better. Use when you've been stuck for more than 20 minutes.

## Prompt

I'm stuck on something. I'll describe what I'm trying to do, what I've tried, and where my thinking breaks down. Don't solve it yet — first, ask me one clarifying question that targets the place I'm most likely to have made a wrong assumption.

After I answer, propose 2–3 directions, ranked by what looks most likely to be the actual issue. For each, say what evidence would confirm or rule it out.

Here's the problem:

{{describe what you're stuck on}}
